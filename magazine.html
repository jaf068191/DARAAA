About DARA

Welcome to DARA — my digital archival experiment. It functions as an online mood board and critical diary documenting the intersections of classic runway fashion, dark synth-pop subcultures, and cult cinematic framing. Built for anyone obsessed with raw aesthetics, timeless soundscapes, and design history.
